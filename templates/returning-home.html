Returning home

When should I return home?

Return to your home when fire officials determine it is safe. Notification that it is safe to return home will be given as soon as possible considering safety and accessibility.


What actions should I take upon returning?

  • Stay alert for downed power lines and other hazards.
  • Check propane tanks, regulators, and lines before turning gas on.
  • Check your residence carefully for hidden embers or smoldering fires.
