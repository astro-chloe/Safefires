Protect agriculture

Fire-resistant zones

To resist the spread of fire to crops and your home, consider creating fire-resistant zones with stone walls, patios, decks and roadways. Use rock, mulch, flower beds and gardens as ground cover for bare spaces and as effective firebreaks.

Fire-resistant plants can also be strategically planted to resist the spread of fire to your home. Consider landscaping your home with these fire-resistant plants: French Lavender, Red Monkey Flower, Sage, Society Garlic, Ornamental Strawberry, Yellow Ice Plant, Coreopsis, California Lilac, California Fuchsia, & California Red Bud.
